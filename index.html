About me

My interest in accessibility stems from my mother and her side of the family, many of whom experience partial hearing loss, including her.
Growing up, I saw the challenges they faced in communication, from struggling to follow conversations in noisy settings to relying on captions and assistive devices.
These experiences made me aware of the barriers that exist for people with hearing impairments and inspired my passion for creating inclusive solutions.
I believe accessibility is essential for ensuring equal opportunities, and I am committed to advocating for tools and technologies that make communication more inclusive for everyone.
